About Me
---

I am currently pursuing a Physics major with a Data Science minor at IISER Pune, along with an online BS degree in Data Science and AI from IIT Guwahati. My interests lie at the intersection of physics, data-driven modelling, and complex systems. 

I am currently working in climate modelling, where I am exploring different graphical models and how they can be used to capture structure in high-dimensional data. My present work focuses on the GODAS dataset, where I am studying the ENSO phenomenon, with an emphasis on understanding its underlying dynamics and building interpretable models that reflect meaningful physical connections. I am working on this under the guidance of Bedartha Goswami at IISER Pune.

---

Research Interests

- Neural networks and deep learning  
- Supervised and unsupervised learning methods  
- Quantum information and computing  
- Climate modelling and Earth system dynamics  
- Convolutional neural networks and NLP modelling
